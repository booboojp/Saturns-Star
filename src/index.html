Saturn's Star
Twinkle twinkle little star,
Trapped in darkness,
You are my star.
Trapped alone in this dark place.
Waiting for you to be my escape.
Twinkle twinkle my little star.
I know how precious you are.
You do not see it,
Blinded by your own light.
Always comforting me,
Deep into the night.
